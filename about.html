About TTN

TTN is an experimental financial media platform that tracks key market indicators in real time (Bitcoin, Ethereum, S&P 500, Nasdaq, Gold, EUR/USD) and aggregates up-to-date financial news from established public sources.

What we do

  • Display live quotes for six major market indicators.
  • Aggregate news from vetted financial sources, always linking back to the original article.
  • Automatically detect tickers mentioned in news stories, so you can jump straight to a chart.
  • Provide interactive charts powered by TradingView for every asset.
  • Offer free financial calculators (compound interest, currency conversion, crypto P&L, loans).

What we don't do

We do not rewrite other outlets' articles or present them as our own content — every news item links back to its original source.

Advertising & affiliate disclosure

TTN may display advertising, including through Google AdSense, and may include affiliate links to third-party brokers and exchanges. If you sign up through one of these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. See our Privacy Policy for details.
